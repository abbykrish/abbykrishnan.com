**[Bay Wheels](https://baywheels.fly.dev/)** is a data visualizer and interactive tool for exploring all Bay Wheels bike-share trips in San Francisco. Built with [deck.gl](https://deck.gl/) and [Maplibre](https://maplibre.org/), it lets you explore trip patterns across the city.

My greatest quality of life improvement this last year was a Bay Wheels membership. It's made me less stressed for time and more appreciative of our beautiful city!
